# Expert Sources for Email Marketing — Playbook Citation Reference

An analysis of each expert's qualifications as a citable authority on the email marketing playbook, supported by sources.

## 1. Ryan Deiss
**Specialty:** Funnel design and conversion and revenue optimisation are areas of expertise.

**The reason I picked him:**- Currently the founder and CEO of The Scalable Company, he founded DigitalMarketer.com, his first internet venture, in 1999.
- Developer of the **Customer Value Optimisation (CVO)** methodology, a methodical framework that is extensively taught and referenced in the industry for converting email into a revenue generator.
Daymond John from Shark Tank has stated in public that his businesses "practically own the internet."
- Offers training to more than 500,000 subscribers and thousands of premium DigitalMarketer members.
Lead teacher for the Email Marketing Mastery certification program offered by DigitalMarketer.

The Customer Value Journey (CVJ) structure, funnel-based email sequencing, and revenue-driven email strategy are all included in the playbook.

**Sources:** LinkedIn, inboxarmy.com expert directory, and digitalmarketer.com (professor page, certificates).

## 2. Val Geisler
**Speciality:** Buyer-Led Positioning, Retention, and Lifecycle Strategy is the speciality.

**The reason I picked her:**
Email marketing and conversion copywriter/strategist with more than ten years of internal experience in SaaS, eCommerce, NGOs, and tech startups, including a period at ConvertKit.
- Has worked closely with well-known software companies and been highlighted by Stripe, Litmus, Trello, and InVision as an email specialist.
Joanna Wiebe, the creator of Copyhackers, has endorsed it as a leading authority on email optimisation services and lifecycle strategy.
Positioning work that is "activated" immediately in lifecycle emails, sales discussions, and retention flows—rather than merely theoretical communications frameworks—is the current focus.

Use in the playbook for messaging-to-copy activation, subscription/MRR-brand email strategy, and lifecycle/retention email sequences.

**Sources:** Valgeisler.com, academy.omniconvert.com, appcues.com, and thecopywriterclub.com.
